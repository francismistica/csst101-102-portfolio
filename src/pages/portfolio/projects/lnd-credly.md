# LND Credly

**LND Credly** is a centralized database designed specifically for members of the Lingkod Ng Dambana SJBP Liliw (LND) community. Our platform provides a seamless and efficient way for members to access, manage, and showcase their certificates and achievements.

## Key Features

-   **Certificate Management**: Securely store and retrieve certificates.
-   **Achievement Showcase**: Display badges and accomplishments.
-   **Centralized Database**: Easy access for all community members.

Whether you have completed a course, attended a workshop, or earned a badge, LND Credly ensures that all your accomplishments are securely stored and easily accessible.

## Tech Stack

Built with:
-   **React** & **Next.js** for a fast, modern frontend.
-   **TailwindCSS** for responsive styling.
-   **Firebase** for the backend and database.
