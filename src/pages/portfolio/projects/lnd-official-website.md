# LND Official Website

The **LND Credly Community** is a platform dedicated to ministry members, focused on organizing events, workshops, and mentoring sessions.

## Key Features

-   **Event Management**: Streamlined organization of workshops and meetups.
-   **Community Hub**: A central place for members to connect and share resources.
-   **Mentorship Programs**: connecting experienced professionals with aspiring developers.

Built with **Astro** for performance and **TailwindCSS** for a modern, responsive design.
